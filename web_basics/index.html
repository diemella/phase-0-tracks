Chocolate Mug Cake

Hey, chocolate cake lovers! Here's an easy recipe you can whip up using the microwave and and enjoy within minutes. This recipe was adapted from a Food Network recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbs all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbs sugar
  • 2 tbs cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbs milk
  • 3 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbs Nutella
